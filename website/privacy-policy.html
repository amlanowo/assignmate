Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how AssignMate handles your data.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

AssignMate is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal personal information:

  • Homework Data: Assignment titles, due dates, subjects, and notes you create
  • App Preferences: Your theme settings, notification preferences, and app configuration
  • Usage Analytics: Basic app usage statistics to improve functionality (anonymized)

ðŸ”’ Local Storage Only

All your homework data is stored locally on your device. We do not sync your personal assignments to any external servers or cloud services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the AssignMate app functionality
  • Send you homework reminders and notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use your homework data to send you timely reminders about upcoming assignments. You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings.

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your homework data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
  • No internet connection is required to access your homework
  • Your assignments remain private and secure

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted local storage on your device
  • Secure data handling practices
  • Regular security updates and patches

4. Third-Party Services

AssignMate may use the following third-party services:

Expo (Development Platform)

We use Expo for app development and distribution. Expo may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve their services. Please review Expo's Privacy Policy for more information.

Google Play Store

If you download AssignMate from Google Play Store, Google may collect information about your download and usage. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all your homework data stored in the app
  • Modify: Edit, update, or delete your homework assignments
  • Export: Export your homework data (feature coming soon)
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to remove all local data
  • Notifications: Enable or disable notifications at any time

ðŸ’¡ Data Control

Since your data is stored locally, you have complete control over it. Uninstalling the app will remove all your homework data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

AssignMate is designed to be safe for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
  • Notifying you through the app if significant changes are made

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
